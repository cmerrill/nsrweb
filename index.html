About Me

Source: PlaceKitten

Hi everybody, I'm Chris! I enjoy fluxing with circuits, tapping out code, drilling down into mechanical design, and taking long walks on the beach while eating lobster rolls.

I graduated from MIT EECS in 2012, and I'm currently working as a Technologist at Massive Health. My job consists of some linear combination of frontend web wet-work and backend bug-patching.
